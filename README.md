# Minion Survivor

A Brotato-inspired browser survival game. Control a potato minion, auto-attack waves of enemies, collect XP, and pick upgrades. **Survive 5 minutes to win!**

## Play

Open `index.html` in a browser, or serve locally:

```bash
python3 -m http.server 8080
```

Then visit http://localhost:8080

## Controls

- **WASD** or **Arrow Keys** — Move
- Attacks are automatic toward the nearest enemy in range
- Click an upgrade card when you level up

## Gameplay

- 5-minute survival timer — survive until 0:00 to win
- Enemies get tougher and spawn faster over time
- Kill enemies for XP orbs; level up to choose from 3 random upgrades
- Enemy types: grunts, speedy, tanks, swarms, and elites (late game)
